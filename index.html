How to Play -

Please select the device where you want to play PhoenixPS and follow the step-by-step instructions.

Virtual Host

1

Download and install Virtual Hosts APK.

2

Click here to download the required hosts file.

3

Open Virtual Hosts and select 'SELECT HOSTS FILE'.

4

Add the hosts file you just downloaded.

5

Activate the switch to start playing on our server.

Power Tunnel

1

Download and install Power Tunnel APK.

2

Click copy host button to copy the hosts link.

3

Open Power Tunnel and click three-line icon.

4

Click host settings icon then paste the hosts link you copied before.

5

Change the host update period from 12 hours to on start.

6

Now you can play on our server!
